MythFlash Uploader

A private tool used by the MythFlash creator to upload their own videos to their own TikTok account.

MythFlash Uploader is a personal command-line utility. It takes short mythology videos produced for the MythFlash channel and sends them to the creator's own TikTok account as drafts, using TikTok's Content Posting API. Every draft is then reviewed and published by hand inside the TikTok app.

It is not a product, not a service, and has no users other than the creator. It does not accept sign-ups, it does not post on anyone else's behalf, and nothing it uploads becomes public without a human pressing Post.
